## ✅ Daily Night Routine

1. **Cleanse** - Use a sulfate-free shampoo (like Traya’s Gentle Cleanser) 2–3x/week. Skip on other days — dry scalp hates over-washing.

2. **Treat** - Apply 6–8 drops of **Traya Hair Growth Serum** directly to scalp. Massage in circular motions for 2 mins (boosts circulation).

3. **Lock In** - Follow with 2 drops of **cold-pressed coconut oil** on ends only. *Never* apply oil to scalp — traps dryness.

4. **Sleep** - Use a silk pillowcase. Cotton sucks moisture. 

## 📆 Weekly Add-On

- **Day 3**: Apply Traya’s **Scalp Nourishing Mask** (15 mins, rinse with cool water). 
- **Day 7**: Skip serum, do a 5-min scalp massage with rosemary water (1 tsp rosemary boiled in 1 cup water, cooled).

- Alcohol-based styling products
- Tight ponytails
